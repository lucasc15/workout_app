LVJ Workouts

By: Lucas, Vu, and Jon

Prog 8110 Final Group Project

Our group project for PROG 8110 will be a workout log, that will allow the user to select exercises, complete workouts, and update the numbers of sets, and weight used. This will give the user a reliable and easy to use way to keep track of their workouts. The goal is to include a wide variety or exercises and potentially include a calories burned section.
